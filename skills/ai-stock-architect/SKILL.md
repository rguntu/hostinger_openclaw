# ai-stock-architect

AI Supply Chain and Moat Research.

## Usage

Use this skill to research and summarize stocks critical to the AI buildout (chips, energy, cooling, data centers). Focus on competitive advantages and strong moats.

## Capabilities

- **Supply Chain Scan:** Analyze specific tiers (Foundry, Design, Power, Thermal).
- **Moat Analysis:** Verify pricing power, switching costs, and high barriers to entry.
- **Morning Briefing:** Generate a structured report for the `#ai-stock-research` channel.

## Protocol

1. Perform a deep web search for the latest sector performance.
2. Filter for 3–5 high-conviction "moat" players.
3. Contrast analyst sentiment against technical/fundamental facts.
4. Output to channel: `1486605655409299558`.
5. Mirror a high-level summary to the main session (Telegram/DM) for immediate awareness.
